      I am a Lecturer in the Department of Information Security, School of Software and Microelectronics, Peking University(PKU). I am a member of Information Security Laboratory and BlockChain Research Center at PKU.

      My research explores Blockchain and Authentication, with current focus on Sharding, Consensus, LightClient and Password.
