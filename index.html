      I am an assistant professor (Lecturer) in the Department of Information System, School of Software and Microelectronics,Peking University(PKU). I am now leading ISE Group in Information Security Laboratory at PKU. I am also a member of CCF, IEEE and ACM.

     My research explores identity management and trust management, with current focus on Authentication, Password, Blockchain and Credit Scoring.
